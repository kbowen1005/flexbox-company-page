Kalyn Bowen

Lead Vocalist | Acoustic Guitar

Wendell Gooch

Vocalist | Lead Acoustic Guitar

Kalyn Bowen and Wendell are two self-taught musicians who clicked, musically, while picking on a porch, in good ole north Georgia, back in the spring of 2019.

Kalyn is a talented singer/song writer, born and raised in Georgia. Wendell, also born and raised in Georgia, has always had an unbelievable ear for music and is a talented guitarist, which compliments Kalyn's amazing vocal skills. Their shared background in bluegrass and folk music, helps to meld their unique take on the latest and classic hits, as well as, original tunes.

Together, they make Rust and Rose.
